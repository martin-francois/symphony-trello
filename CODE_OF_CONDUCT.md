# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free project space for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but own the impact of your words and actions.
- Keep feedback focused on the work.
- Respect boundaries around private credentials, private project details, and personal information.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Sexualized language or unwanted attention.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Repeated disruption of issues, pull requests, or discussions.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or delete comments; close issues or pull requests; block participants;
or take other moderation actions needed to protect the project space.

If you need to report a conduct concern, contact the maintainers privately through GitHub. Include
links, screenshots, or timestamps when they help explain the concern.
